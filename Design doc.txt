## Brand & Style

This design system is built to evoke the feeling of a high-end, futuristic mentorship experience. It targets ambitious professionals who seek a cutting-edge tool that feels both technologically superior and approachable. 

The aesthetic is a sophisticated blend of **Glassmorphism** and **Minimalism**. By utilizing deep, infinite blacks and layered translucency, the UI suggests depth and intelligence. The emotional response should be one of calm confidence—reducing interview anxiety through a clean, focused, and "magical" interface that feels like a glimpse into the future of career coaching.

## Colors

The palette is anchored in a "Deep Space" dark mode. 
- **Primary:** A vibrant Purple (#A855F7) used for primary actions and AI-driven insights.
- **Secondary:** An Electric Blue (#3B82F6) used for secondary interactive elements and progress indicators.
- **Neutrals:** A scale of deep charcoals. The absolute background is #0A0A0B, while elevated surfaces use #121214 with varying levels of transparency.
- **Gradients:** Use the `accent_gradient` for high-impact areas like active states, premium features, and AI "thinking" animations. 
- **Neon Highlights:** Subtle cyan (#22D3EE) is reserved for critical status indicators or "live" interview signals.

## Typography

This design system utilizes **Geist** for its technical precision and clean, modern SaaS aesthetic. 

- **Hierarchy:** Dramatic contrast between display sizes and body text creates an editorial feel.
- **Letter Spacing:** Headlines use slightly negative tracking for a tighter, premium look. Body and label text use generous letter spacing to ensure maximum readability against dark backgrounds.
- **Weights:** Use 'Regular' (400) for long-form content and 'SemiBold' (600) or 'Bold' (700) for headers and emphasis to ensure clear information architecture.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy follows a **Fluid Grid** model with high emphasis on whitespace to prevent cognitive overload during stressful interview simulations.

- **Grid:** A 12-column system for desktop, transitioning to a 4-column system for mobile.
- **Rhythm:** An 8px base unit governs all dimensions. 
- **Safe Zones:** Use generous 48px margins on desktop to allow the glass panels to "breathe" against the deep background.
- **Reflow:** On mobile, glass cards should stack vertically, losing their horizontal margins to maximize screen real estate for text-based questions.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth is achieved through **Glassmorphism** rather than traditional shadows.

1.  **Backdrop Blur:** All cards must use a 12px to 20px blur radius to separate content from the background.
2.  **Translucency:** Surface layers use #FFFFFF at 3-5% opacity.
3.  **Inner Glows:** Elements should feature a 1px solid white stroke at 10% opacity on the top and left edges to simulate a light source from above.
4.  **Interactive Glow:** Primary buttons and active input fields utilize an outer "neon" bloom—a soft, colored shadow (0px 0px 20px) matching the element's primary color at 30% opacity.

## Shapes

The design system uses a very soft shape language to feel friendly and inviting.

- **Standard Elements:** Buttons and small inputs use 0.5rem (8px).
- **Glass Cards:** Use a "2xl" treatment of 1.5rem (24px) to emphasize the "friendly AI" persona.
- **Interactive States:** On hover, cards may slightly expand, and the corner radius remains constant to maintain structural integrity.
- **Icons:** Should be encased in circular or highly rounded containers to match the overall softness.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid gradient fill (Purple to Blue). On hover, apply a soft glow effect and a 2px upward translation.
- **Secondary:** Glass background with a 1px border.
- **Ghost:** No background, purple text, becomes semi-opaque on hover.

### Glass Cards
- Semi-transparent #121214 background (80% opacity).
- 1px border with a subtle gradient (White 10% to White 2%).
- 24px internal padding.

### Input Fields
- Dark, recessed background (#050505).
- 1px border that glows electric blue upon focus.
- Monospaced font for placeholder text to lean into the "technical" feel.

### Chips & Badges
- Used for "Interview Topic" or "Difficulty Level." 
- Pill-shaped, with a subtle 5% purple tint and 100% opacity text.

### Interactive Feedback
- AI "thinking" state: A shimmering gradient animation that moves across the border of the glass card.
- Feedback highlights: Soft green for "Good Answer" and soft amber for "Improvement Needed," using low-saturation glows rather than harsh solid colors.
